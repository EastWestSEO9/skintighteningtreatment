What is non-surgical skin tightening and how does it work?

Non-surgical skin tightening uses energy-based technologies such as radiofrequency or ultrasound to deliver controlled heat into the deeper layers of the skin. This heat stimulates the body's natural collagen and elastin production, resulting in firmer, tighter and more youthful-looking skin over time. At Solea Brickell Spa in Miami, our non-invasive skin tightening treatments are comfortable, safe and require no downtime whatsoever.

How many hyperpigmentation treatment sessions will I need?

The number of sessions needed for hyperpigmentation treatment depends on the type, severity and location of your pigmentation as well as your skin type and tone. Most clients at our Miami skin care clinic begin to see noticeable improvement after 3 to 6 sessions. We will give you a clear, honest timeline after your initial skin assessment so you know exactly what to expect from your treatment plan.

Is melasma treatment safe for all skin types?

Yes. At Solea Brickell Spa, our melasma treatment protocols are specifically customized to accommodate all skin types and tones, including deeper complexions that require particular care. We use clinically proven methods that minimize the risk of adverse reactions while effectively reducing the appearance of melasma patches. We are especially experienced in treating the diverse skin tones represented in Miami's multicultural population.

How long do skin tightening results typically last?

Results from our non-invasive skin tightening services can last anywhere from 1 to 3 years depending on your individual skin characteristics, age, lifestyle and home care routine. Most clients at our Miami clinic benefit from periodic maintenance sessions to sustain their results and continue building collagen over time. We will guide you on the ideal maintenance schedule for your specific treatment plan.

Can dark spot removal be done on all skin tones?

Absolutely. Our dark spot removal treatments are designed to be effective and safe across the full spectrum of skin tones.
